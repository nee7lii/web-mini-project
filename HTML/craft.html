CRAFTED BETWEEN MOUNTAIN AND HOME

Inside our rug studio

BeniCraft rugs are designed in Morocco and woven by hand in small ateliers. Here, raw wool, color, and tradition come together to create pieces made to live with you for years.

HANDWOVEN IN OUR ATELIER

A living workshop, not a factory

BeniCraft's studio sits between the mountains and the city — a quiet place where light, wool, and hands come together. Inside, looms stand tall, yarn hangs in soft palettes, and every tool carries the rhythm of craft passed down through generations.

We work in small, focused teams, each artisan contributing a chapter to the life of a rug. Nothing here is rushed. Nothing is automated. Every piece is shaped slowly, intentionally, and with the kind of care that machines can't imitate.

OUR PROCESS

A craft rooted in patience

Wool selection

We begin with high-grade wool sourced from the Atlas region, chosen for its softness, strength, and natural warmth. Only the best fibers make it into our rugs.

Carding & spinning

The wool is hand-carded and spun into yarn using time-honored techniques. This slow preparation gives each strand its character and resilience.

Natural dyeing

Our dyers work with earthy palettes and small-batch color. Subtle shifts in tone mean no two rugs are exactly the same, giving each piece a quiet uniqueness.

Hand weaving

On traditional looms, artisans build the rug line by line. Weeks of patient work are woven into every piece, so it can live with you for years, not seasons.

TECHNIQUES

The art behind every fiber

Every BeniCraft rug is shaped through a series of intentional, time-honored techniques carried by artisans who have practiced this craft for generations.

Weaving Structure

Built on traditional looms, the foundation structure of each rug determines its durability, softness, and signature feel.

Natural Fibers

High-grade wool is chosen for its warmth, strength, and organic texture, giving every piece a natural depth that synthetic fibers can't match.

Hand Finishing

Edges are trimmed, washed, and softened by hand, allowing the rug to reveal its final character and settle into its long life.

OUR ATELIER

The heart of our craft

Inside our quiet atelier, artisans work patiently and intentionally, shaping every rug with a rhythm passed down through generations. This is where wool becomes art.
